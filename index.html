Hello!
I'm Summer and I like making video games!
Resume Projects

I am a game designer specializing in player interactions and experiences. I'm a detail-oriented developer that can see possible future problems and their solutions before they even appear and am not afraid to tackle those challenges head on. When I conquer those challenges, I take detailed notes and learn from the experience so I, and my team, can avoid those challenges in the future.

Currently, I'm working as a Software Developer in Unity at UCSim | Center for Simulations & Virtual Realities.

My goal is to always prioritize player experiences and help develop systems that elevate the narrative experiences...or just make sure that the players have fun and get to escape the real world for a little while. I like creating and seeing the results in real-time, so shaders, gameplay, and interactions are my favorite. I want to work towards becoming a Lead Gameplay Designer where I can design unique narrative experiences or a Technical Designer where I can create shaders, tools, and otherwise be as much of an asset to my team as I can be.

I've worked with Unity since high school and have been using it daily since I started at UCSim in 2020. There, I am one of 4 developers creating tools, functionality, and interactions in AR and VR experiences for internal and external industry partners. I'm never afraid to take initiative and acquire skills our team needs, whether that's understanding Unity's new networking system or teaching myself HLSL and shader graph.

When I'm not working, I'm likely teaching myself a new language (I'm comfortable in French and am currently working on Korean), reading my next fantasy novel, or DMing my homebrew D&D campaign set in the Dragon Age universe .
