Currently Working On - Model for Belladonna
Currently, I'm working on modeling the player character for the game a small team of friends and I have been working on for a few years.

I've modeled this character a number of times over the years but this time, with the help of Synty assets, we've begun more of a solid base. I've begun this model in the similar design of Synty assets so I can have custom assets while also being able to stick to the style of the pre-existing assets. I'm going to be incorporating physics into this character in the form of the braid (making it dynamic when the player moves) and the tail that is going to be coming off the end of the outfit.
I plan on rigging and painting this character as well. After that, I plan on importing the characters and continue work on the foundational mechanics and controls for the player character.

Blood Traveler
The first game I created. It follows a character that slips into a portal one day and finds herself in the body of her future ancestor. She must find her way home using narrative puzzles and platforming.

I created the locomotion, animations (using Mixamo), AI behavior and pathing, narrative sequencing, behaviors, and interactions. My partner created the story, cinematics, levels, and platforming.
