Blood Traveler
The first game I created. It follows a character that slips into a portal one day and finds herself in the body of her future ancestor. She must find her way home using narrative puzzles and platforming.

I created the locomotion, animations (using mixamo), ai behavior and pathing, narrative sequencing, behaviors, and interactions. My partner created the story, cinematics, levels, and platforming.
