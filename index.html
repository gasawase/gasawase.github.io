Hi! I'm Summer!
and I like building video games

I am a game designer specializing in player interactions and experiences. Currently, I'm working as a Software Developer in Unity at UCSim | Center for Simulations & Virtual Realities. My goal is to always prioritize player experiences and help develop systems that elevate the narrative experiences...or just make sure that the players have fun and get to escape the real world for a little while. I want to work towards becoming a Lead Gameplay Designer where I can design unique narrative experiences or a Technical Designer where I can be as much of an asset to my team as I can be.

I've worked with Unity since high school and have been using it daily since I started at UCSim in 2020. There, I am one of 4 developers creating tools, functionality, and interactions in AR and VR experiences for internal and external industry partners. I'm never afraid to take initiative and acquire skills our team needs, whether that's understanding Unity's new networking system or teaching myself shader code.

When I'm not working, I'm likely teaching myself a new language (I'm comfortable in French and am currently working on Korean), reading my next fantasy novel, or DMing my homebrew D&D campaign.
