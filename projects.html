Multiplayer Demo
This is a multiplayer game where players work together to defeat an enemy in a gladiator ring. It creates a session when a host is selected, tracks the players and their names when they're spawned, networks the animations, locations, and health of the players, and tracks the stats for the enemy.

I was the only developer on this project.
Problems I had:
• Having Unity change its multiplayer framework halfway through development
• Wrapping my head around server and client rpcs
How I got out of it:
• Reading lots of documentation, asking for help, Googling, and good old trial and error
• Testing and testing and testing until it clicked for me

Hope After Hate: Virtual Reality Experience
Hope After Hate was one of the first projects I'd worked on for longer than a year and that I saw from its midpoint to the end of the second deployment. I was first a minor Software Developer, but eventually was brought on to handle entire scenes. There was even a point where I was the only one on my team working on it so I had to become well aquainted with how it worked and couldn't rely on my supervisor for help.
Problems I had:
• Working with custom scripting and 360 video that constantly broke
• Balance game mechanics (such as tutorial) with emotional storytelling of narrative
How I got out of it:
• Worked with my team to try and rebuild the scripting or at least fix it to a point where the player wouldn't notice the problems
• Redid the previous tutorial to take place in the environment, pushed for more realistic graphics, and advocated for utilization for music
