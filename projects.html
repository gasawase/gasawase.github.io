Multiplayer Demo
This is a multiplayer game using Unity's Netcode for Game Objects and Synty Studio assets where players work together to defeat an enemy in a gladiator ring. It creates a session when a host is selected, tracks the players and their names when they're spawned, networks the animations, locations, and health of the players, and tracks the stats for the enemy.

I was the only developer on this project.

Blood Traveler
BloodTraveler is a short narrative action RPG using Unreal 4 and assets from Synty Studios. It follows the main character as they fall through a portal through time. They wake up in the distant future in the body of their ancestor. The player must find a way to get back to their own time before they lose themselves.

It utilizes dialogue trees, cutscenes, AI pathing, quest progression, and item interactions.
Hope After Hate: Virtual Reality Experience
Hope After Hate was one of the first projects I'd worked on for longer than a year and that I saw from its midpoint to the end of the second deployment. I was first a Software Developer, but eventually was brought on to handle entire scenes. There was even a point where I was the only one on my team working on it so I had to become well aquainted with how it worked and couldn't rely on my supervisor for help.
RepairBase
For my Senior Design Project, my team and I decided to create a VR Simulation that taught the user how to fix common laptop issues. I was very familiar with VR simulations as I had been developing them for two years at that point. I took on the role of Lead Software Developer and Project Manager.
